Our Story

More Than Coffee.
We Build Community.

Nestled in Times Square Plaza on the Gulf to Lake Highway, Inverness Coffee House is the kind of place where your order gets remembered after the second visit. RJ and Rob Cummins opened this shop with a single mission: make every person who walks through the door feel like they belong here.

The menu keeps evolving — crepes, açaí bowls, Lotus energy drinks, hand-spun soft serve, and coffee crafted with real care. But the soul stays the same.

"You've got to constantly evolve, constantly grow. If you're not growing, you're dying." — RJ Cummins

"We're not just serving coffee — we're building relationships."

— RJ Cummins, Owner
